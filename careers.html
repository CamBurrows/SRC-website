Careers at SRC

WANTED! Experienced, people-minded, profit driven, motivated people who don't mind going home with that distinct "flame broiled smell." We are always looking for enthusiastic managers!

All that talent, all that ambition – Burger King (SRC) is somewhere you can really bring it, every single day. We’re a business that’s 100% built on the energy and hunger of its people. Join our management team, you’ll never be short of opportunities to show what you’ve got. And if we like what we see, there’s no limit to how far you could go here.

We’re an ambitious business, and we like our people to be the same. You’ll be expected to bring it each and every day. That means raising your game within a high-performing team that’s driven by results. It means always doing your best and always giving your all. And it means welcoming new responsibilities that stretch and challenge you – even when they take you far beyond your comfort zone. So we expect a lot of our people. You’ll need extraordinary levels of ownership, drive, and accountability to join the team here. Demonstrate all that and you’ll quickly discover that this is an environment where great results and great rewards go hand in hand. It’s not a regular job for sure. But if you’re looking for the chance to really make something of yourself, that’s exactly what you’ll find at Burger King (SRC).
