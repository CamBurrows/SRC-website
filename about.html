About Us

Savannah RestAurants corporation

Savannah Restaurants Corporation is a franchisee of Burger King Corporation. The company was formed in 1986 by William T. deLaet and Alex Salgueiro when they purchased the original seven BK restaurants in Savannah. We are committed to the goal of operating restaurants at the highest level of excellence to provide our customers a quality product, served fast, in spotlessly clean, pleasant surroundings by neat, courteous, happy people.

If you join the team, you will become our main contact with our customers. Our excellent track record, combined with the strength of the Burger King® brand, offers prospective employees a rewarding career opportunity.

Burger King ®

Burger King® Corporation is the world's second largest quick-service hamburger chain, with over 11,100 restaurants operating in 65 countries and international territories around the world and in all 50 states. Burger King® Corporation is an innovative leader in today's competitive and growing quick service restaurant industry.
