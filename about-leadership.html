Leadership Team

Alex Salgueiro

President / CEO

Mr. Salgueiro was born in Havana, Cuba. He immigrated to Miami, Florida in 1960 with his family where he grew up. He attended school in Miami through graduation and later received an A.A. degree from Miami Dade Community College and a Bachelor’s Degree from Florida International University.

He began his career with Burger King in 1970 while in high school as a part-time employee. Through a scholarship from Burger King Corporation, he worked full time as a Manager and attended college. During his career with Burger King, he held many positions, the most interesting being in International Franchising. In this position, he developed and opened Burger King Restaurants in several countries including England, Spain, Denmark, Sweden, Venezuela, Columbia, Panama, Ecuador, Aruba, Grand Cayman and Puerto Rico.

In 1981, Mr. Salgueiro relocated to Atlanta, Georgia, and became the Region Manager of the Atlanta Region until he retired from Burger King Corporation in 1986. While in Atlanta, he met and married his wife, Fonda Maulden from Montgomery, Alabama. On June 1, 1986, they moved to Savannah to become Burger King Franchisees.
